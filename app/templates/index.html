Market Sentiment Analyser

This web-tool that goes through the new posts and comments from r/wallstreetbets and financial news sources (CNBC, Reuters, Investing, Economic Times, Google' aggregated financial news). It finds the subjects using NLP library called spacy and calculates the sentiment of the subject in the posts and comments. The sentiment is calculated using the VADER sentiment analysis tool and is totalled over the number of posts and comments specified by the user.

NOTE: The higher the number of posts and comments being analysed, the longer it takes to retrieve the comments and posts from reddit
